### üöÄ How this becomes a fully public, functional website

To make this site live and accessible to everyone, the following steps happen:

1. **Finalize design & content**

   * Confirm layout, branding, and pages
2. **Connect a custom domain**

   * Purchase or attach a domain name
3. **Enable production deployment**

   * Switch from demo/sandbox to live publishing
4. **Turn on public access**

   * Allow search engines and external visitors
5. **Optional upgrades**

   * Contact forms
   * User accounts
   * CMS editing
   * Analytics
   * Performance optimization

At that point, this becomes a **fully functional, real website** that anyone can visit and interact with.
